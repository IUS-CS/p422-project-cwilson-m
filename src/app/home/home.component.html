Home insurance

Home insurance is very different from either boat or auto insurance simply because of the many different ways you have to classify it.

I have broken it down into 2 different categories.

Those being Home with a basement and without a basement.

These prices are for general homeowners insurance.
There are many other things you need to protect your home such as fire, tornado, water, and many other types.
Your prices are listed below.

A 1500 square foot home without a basement would normally cost around 1000 per year.

A 2400 square foot home without a basement would normally cost around 1850 per year.

A 3000 square foot home without a basement would normally cost around 2200 per year.

A 1500 square foot home with a basement would normally cost around 1200 per year.

A 2400 square foot home with a basement would normally cost around 2175 per year.

A 3000 square foot home with a basement would normally cost around 2550 per year.
